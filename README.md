# sudoku
Sudoku puzzle
this is a sudoku puzzle, where there are missing cells on a 9 by 9 board. your goal is to fill in the numbers so that each row, column, and the mini square has to contain numbers 1 to 9, all numbers only once. more than one number cannot be placed on the same column, row, or the mini square. (the mini square is the small 3 by 3 grid inside the 9 by 9 grid, the sides are bolded a bit, and there are 9 of them in the sudoku board)
you can also customize difficulties (please dont play troll, thats just to troll you) i cant guarantee that the very hard puzzles are solvable without using brute force
after you genearte a puzzle, please click the print button and you will have your sudoku puzzle (you cant type on it sorry, you will have to print it or copy it on a piece of paper)
